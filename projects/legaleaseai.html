Technical Architecture

Unlike standard wrappers, LegalEase utilizes a strict pipeline to prevent dangerous legal hallucinations:

  • 1. Extraction: `pdfplumber` and `PyPDF2`
  • 2. Chunking: `LangChain` splitters feeding a `FAISS` vector DB.
  • 3. Risk Detection: Keyword and semantic pattern matching.
  • 4. Generative Explanations: Prompt-engineered `GPT` or `Gemini` models returning structured Urdu JSON.
  • 5. Automated Off-boarding: Formatted `ReportLab` and `WeasyPrint` PDF generations.
💬 Grounded RAG

Ask About Your Document

Interact directly with your contract. The chatbot constraints answers strictly to context found within the uploaded document using Vector Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). Every answer provides its clause citation, ensuring complete transparency.

📊 Deliverables

Compiled Risk Reports

Instantly view aggregated statistics tracking Safe, Medium, and High-Risk clauses. Download beautifully formatted complete PDF reports directly via the browser for physical review.
